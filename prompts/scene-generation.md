# Scene Generation Prompts

## Master Scene Prompt

Hand-painted top-down action RPG scene in a mythological Mediterranean world inspired by ancient Greece. Bright turquoise sea, warm sunlight, lush vegetation, overgrown ruins, atmospheric depth, painterly textures, cinematic lighting, exploration-focused composition, nature reclaiming ancient civilization, small lone adventurer, sense of wonder, freedom, mystery and discovery. Inspired by Secret of Mana and Chrono Trigger. Premium indie RPG art direction. Not grimdark. Not cartoonish. Not heavily anime.

Hand-painted top-down action RPG scene in a mythological Mediterranean world inspired by ancient Greece.
