# Creature Generation Prompts

## Creature Concept Template

Design a mythological creature for Aegea, a top-down action RPG set in a sunlit mythological Mediterranean world inspired by ancient Greece. Creature habitat: [HABITAT]. Role: [AMBIENT / ENEMY / GUARDIAN / GUIDE / BOSS]. Use regionally grounded materials and motifs such as marble, bronze, roots, flowers, shells, salt, reeds, feathers, sunlight, or black water. The creature should have a readable top-down silhouette, ancient mythic presence, and environmental connection. Not grimdark, not cartoonish, not generic medieval fantasy, not heavily anime.

## Required Output Fields

- Name.
- Habitat.
- Silhouette.
- Materials and colors.
- Behavior.
- Combat or interaction role.
- Environmental storytelling purpose.
- Animation notes.
- Sound notes.

## Example Prompt

Design a guardian creature for a cracked coastal temple in Aegea. It should feel ancient and sacred, with bronze, salt, marble, gull feathers, and sea-worn texture. It guards a shrine door, moves with deliberate ritual weight, and remains readable from top-down gameplay scale. Tone: mysterious, dangerous, beautiful, hopeful.
