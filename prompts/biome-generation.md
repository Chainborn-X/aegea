# Biome Generation Prompts

## Template

Create a cohesive biome concept for Aegea, a top-down action RPG in a mythological Mediterranean world inspired by ancient Greece. Biome: [BIOME]. Include terrain, vegetation, lighting, landmarks, traversal hooks, discovery hooks, color palette, ambient life, mythological influence, and how nature reclaims civilization. Tone: wonder, solitude, adventure, mystery, hope. Avoid grimdark, cartoonish presentation, heavy anime influence, and generic medieval fantasy.

## Biome Checklist

- Primary terrain forms.
- Region-specific plants.
- Water, weather, or sky behavior.
- Ruin and architecture language.
- Player scale cues.
- At least three discovery hooks.
- At least one distant landmark.
- Combat and non-combat encounter ideas.
- Palette notes.
- Audio mood.

## Starter Biomes

- Coastal regions.
- Sea caves.
- Forests.
- Sacred groves.
- Marshlands.
- Mountains.
- Ancient temples.
- Underworld regions.
- Forgotten villages.
- Cliffside ruins.
