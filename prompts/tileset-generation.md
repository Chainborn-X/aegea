# Tileset Generation Prompts

## Tileset Template

Create a hand-painted top-down action RPG tileset for Aegea, a mythological Mediterranean world inspired by ancient Greece. Tileset biome: [BIOME]. Include ground tiles, edge transitions, path tiles, water or terrain features, vegetation, ruin fragments, props, discovery hooks, and collision-readable boundaries. Painterly textures, warm sunlight, strong readability, premium indie RPG art direction. Nature reclaiming ancient civilization. Not grimdark, not cartoonish, not heavily anime, not generic medieval fantasy.

## Required Tile Groups

- Base terrain.
- Terrain transitions.
- Path and worn travel surfaces.
- Water or elevation boundaries where relevant.
- Vegetation clusters.
- Ruin fragments.
- Interactive props.
- Secret or discovery markers.
- Large landmark pieces.
