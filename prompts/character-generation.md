# Character Generation Prompts

## NPC Template

Design an NPC for Aegea, a top-down action RPG in a mythological Mediterranean world inspired by ancient Greece. Region: [REGION]. Role: [ROLE]. The character should feel grounded in local landscape, work, ritual, and history. Use practical clothing, Mediterranean fabric shapes, subtle mythic details, and a clear top-down silhouette. Tone: quiet, mysterious, human, hopeful. Avoid heavy anime influence, cartoonish exaggeration, generic medieval fantasy, and modern fashion.

## Player Costume Template

Design an outfit variation for the lone traveler in Aegea. The outfit should support exploration in [REGION], remain readable from top-down view, and communicate growth without turning the character into an overdesigned hero. Use Mediterranean materials, weathering, travel gear, and subtle relic details. Tone: solitary, adventurous, sunlit, mythic.

## Required Notes

- Region and social context.
- Silhouette.
- Materials.
- Palette.
- Top-down readability.
- Animation considerations.
- Relationship to the emotional core.
