# Vision Art Manifest

These images are vision art, not production-ready tilesets.

They answer:

> What should Aegea feel like?

They do not yet answer:

> How do we build Aegea from reusable tiles, props, and modules?

## Files

- `aegea-vision-coast-cave-collage.png`
  Initial mood piece showing coastal exploration, ruins, beach/shipwreck atmosphere, and cave mystery.

- `aegea-biome-vision-grid.png`
  Biome mood grid showing multiple environment directions for Aegea, including coast, ruins, marsh, cave, forest, temples, and mythic landscapes.

## Generated Scene Additions

These standalone scenes extend the same visual language as the original vision art: painterly top-down action RPG perspective, small lone adventurer scale, lush Mediterranean vegetation, ancient ruins, readable traversal paths, and discovery hooks.

- `aegea-vision-sunken-harbor.png`
  Flooded ancient harbor with submerged quays, broken columns, turquoise water, and a distant island shrine.

- `aegea-vision-oracle-spring.png`
  Sacred spring courtyard with glowing water, statues, overgrown marble, and hidden paths through trees.

- `aegea-vision-olive-grove-shrine.png`
  Sunlit hillside shrine surrounded by olive trees, votive lamps, roots, flowers, and terrace paths.

- `aegea-vision-cliff-bridge-ruins.png`
  High cliff bridge ruin above sea mist, with distant temple approach and strong vertical scale.

- `aegea-vision-moonlit-sea-cave.png`
  Night sea cave with moonlit water, cyan crystals, torch contrast, stepping-stone traversal, and shrine doors.

- `aegea-vision-underworld-river-gate.png`
  Beautiful underworld river threshold with black stone terraces, cyan river light, braziers, and monumental gates.

- `aegea-vision-mountain-temple-approach.png`
  Mountain stair path above clouds leading toward a temple, with cypress, statues, bells, and altitude scale.

- `aegea-vision-abandoned-fishing-village.png`
  Quiet coastal fishing village reclaimed by flowers and ivy, with harbor views and hidden routes.

- `aegea-vision-stormlit-coastal-watchtower.png`
  Storm-cleared coastal watchtower with wet stone, beacon fire, wave spray, and hopeful sunlight.
