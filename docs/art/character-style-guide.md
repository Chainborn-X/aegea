# Character Style Guide

## Player Character

The player should read as a lone traveler, not a chosen-one spectacle at first glance.

Use:

- Practical clothing suited for travel.
- Mediterranean fabric shapes and colors.
- Clear top-down silhouette.
- Subtle mythic details that can grow over time.

Avoid:

- Overdesigned armor.
- Modern anime facial language.
- Oversized weapons unless earned through gameplay.
- Generic knight or wizard shorthand.

## NPCs

NPC designs should reflect place, work, and local tradition.

Examples:

- Shore keepers with salt-worn cloaks and shell charms.
- Grove herbalists with laurel, linen, and woven baskets.
- Mountain guards with bronze pins, weathered cloaks, and rope.
- Island craftspeople with terracotta, dyed cloth, and tools.

## Expression

Characters can be warm, strange, guarded, generous, afraid, or hopeful. Avoid broad cartoon exaggeration.
