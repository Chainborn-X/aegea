# Color Language

## Primary Palette Families

- Sea: turquoise, deep blue-green, bright foam, sunlit cyan.
- Stone: warm marble, ivory, pale limestone, dusty ochre, weathered gray.
- Vegetation: olive, cypress green, laurel, moss, wildflower accents.
- Earth: terracotta, dry grass, warm soil, cliff red, sun-baked clay.
- Sacred accents: gold, faded ultramarine, mineral green, flower pink, ritual white.

## Lighting

Outdoor scenes should use warm sunlight with cool reflected shadows. Water should actively contribute color to nearby stone and cave interiors.

## Regional Variation

- Coast: high contrast between pale stone and turquoise water.
- Forest: layered greens with warm shafts of light.
- Marsh: greens, reeds, reflective water, mist, and muted gold.
- Mountain: pale stone, dry grasses, cool shadows, and strong sky color.
- Underworld: desaturated stone, dim gold, pale flowers, black water, and blue-gray depth.

## Avoid

- Muddy brown dominance.
- Overly dark blue fantasy palettes.
- Neon saturation without atmospheric reason.
- Monochrome region design.
- Purple-blue magical shorthand as the default.
