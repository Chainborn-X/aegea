# Creature Style Guide

## Top-Down Readability

Creature designs must be readable from gameplay distance. Prioritize silhouette, movement profile, and color blocking before fine detail.

## Materials And Motifs

Use regionally grounded motifs:

- Sea: shells, salt, foam, coral-like growth, wet scales.
- Grove: bark, flowers, roots, pollen, leaf shapes.
- Temple: marble, bronze, ritual paint, cracked stone.
- Marsh: reeds, mud, reflective skin, pale eyes.
- Mountain: horns, stone plates, wind-torn feathers, dry grasses.
- Underworld: ash, pale flowers, dim gold, black water, bone-white stone.

## Personality

Not every creature should be aggressive. Some should observe, flee, guide, block, mimic, sing, gather, or protect.

## Avoid

- Generic fantasy enemy sets.
- Joke monsters.
- Excessive gore.
- Designs that only make sense in side view.
