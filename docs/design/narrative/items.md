# Items

## Item Design Rules

- Major items should be authored, not randomized.
- Items should have lore, gameplay purpose, and discovery context.
- Relics should change traversal, combat, puzzle-solving, and world interpretation.
- Consumables should support adventure without turning the game into loot management.

## Weapons

### Bronze Shortblade

Lore: Common village blade.

Gameplay purpose: Basic sword.

Discovery method: Given by Demeas during the opening crisis.

### Kopis of the First Oath

Lore: Pactbearer weapon that remembers prior wielders.

Gameplay purpose: Main upgradeable sword.

Discovery method: Pactbearer crypt beneath Elia.

### Spear of the Sea Road

Lore: Used by sailors to mark sacred currents.

Gameplay purpose: Longer reach and underwater switch activation.

Discovery method: Thalassion side story.

### Moonbow

Lore: Artemis relic that fires silent crescent arrows.

Gameplay purpose: Ranged combat, moon switches, stealth pulls.

Discovery method: Moonroot Labyrinth.

### Keraunos Edge

Lore: Blade tempered in Zeus's first lightning.

Gameplay purpose: Late-game charged attacks and storm seals.

Discovery method: Storm Crown.

## Armor

### Traveler's Tunic

Lore: Practical clothing from Elia.

Gameplay purpose: Baseline defense.

Discovery method: Starting gear.

### Lionheart Aegis

Lore: Shield made from the oath of Kartha Nemea.

Gameplay purpose: Blocks heavy attacks and reflects roars.

Discovery method: Lion Vault.

### River Mantle

Lore: Woven by mourners to help souls cross.

Gameplay purpose: Protects against memory loss and spirit damage.

Discovery method: Underworld questline.

### Bronze-Wing Harness

Lore: Daedalan glider rig.

Gameplay purpose: Glide over gaps and ride wind streams.

Discovery method: Bronze Labyrinth.

## Relics

### Ember of Hestia

Lore: The first hearth-flame remembered by mortals.

Gameplay purpose: Light braziers, reveal domestic memories, burn roots.

Discovery method: Hearthwood Shrine.

### Conch of Tides

Lore: A shell that remembers the sea before Poseidon ruled it.

Gameplay purpose: Change water levels, call sea paths, calm whirlpools.

Discovery method: Temple of the Drowned Trident.

### Moonbow Charm

Lore: A silver charm from the first pact between hunters and beasts.

Gameplay purpose: Reveal moon doors, commune with animals, support silent traversal.

Discovery method: Moonroot Labyrinth.

### Gauntlet of Hephaestus

Lore: Forge tool built for repair, later misused for war.

Gameplay purpose: Move heavy objects, break walls, operate machinery.

Discovery method: Bronze Labyrinth.

### Lyre of Dawn

Lore: Apollo's healing instrument, missing the string of certainty.

Gameplay purpose: Heal Echo wounds, alter time-of-day mechanisms, awaken songs.

Discovery method: Sunken Library of Apollo.

### Obol of Hades

Lore: A coin accepted by every honest river.

Gameplay purpose: Speak with dead, cross spirit bridges, resist Lethe.

Discovery method: Palace Beneath the River.

### Bolt of Keraunos

Lore: A fragment of Zeus's first thunder before kingship hardened it.

Gameplay purpose: Power ancient pylons, break divine seals, redirect lightning.

Discovery method: Storm Crown.

## Artifacts

### Broken Meridian Shard

Lore: Piece of the machine that caused the Sundering.

Gameplay purpose: Late-game key to Olympus.

Discovery method: Meridian Bastion.

### First Name Tablet

Lore: Records the original pact before gods became rulers.

Gameplay purpose: Unlocks secret ending path.

Discovery method: Requires all language fragments.

### Prometheus's Coal

Lore: A coal that burns without divine permission.

Gameplay purpose: Allows relic use in anti-magic zones.

Discovery method: Hidden mountain trial.

## Consumables

- **Honey Cakes**: restore health.
- **Pomegranate Seeds**: revive once in Underworld areas.
- **Olive Oil**: temporary weapon enhancement.
- **Salt Vials**: repel spirits.
- **Laurel Tea**: restore spirit or relic energy.
- **Memory Wax**: mark one hidden clue on the map.

## Quest Items

- Widow's Bell.
- Drowned Sister's Shell.
- False Hero Crest.
- Automaton Heart-Key.
- Oracle's Unwritten Page.
- Nameless Grave Flower.

Quest items should belong to specific stories and should not become generic inventory clutter.

## Legendary Equipment

### Aegis of the New Dawn

Unlock condition: Resolve all major god arcs.

Gameplay purpose: Converts blocked damage into relic energy.

### Blade Without a Name

Unlock condition: Commit to the end-the-gods path.

Gameplay purpose: Strong against divine enemies, weak against Primordial ones.

### Crown of No King

Unlock condition: Secret ending route.

Gameplay purpose: Prevents forced ending alignment and opens the Eighth Echo path.
