# Player Progression

## Progression Philosophy

Echoes of Olympus avoids traditional MMO leveling. Growth comes from capability, knowledge, exploration, divine blessings, and mastery.

The player should become stronger because they understand the world more deeply.

## Core Growth Tracks

### Relics

Each major dungeon grants a relic that expands:

- combat options
- puzzle vocabulary
- world traversal
- narrative access
- secret discovery

Relics are the primary progression gates.

### Health and Spirit

Health and spirit increase through:

- heart vessels from major bosses
- spirit vessels from restoring memories
- optional shrine trials
- major companion arc resolutions

### Weapon Mastery

The player learns techniques from:

- Hero Shades
- training stones
- companions
- hidden scrolls
- boss rematches or memory trials

Example techniques:

- dash slash
- charged thrust
- parry counter
- spin attack
- relic combo attacks
- shield roar reflection
- moonshot stun

### Knowledge

Knowledge is progression.

Learning a language or myth truth can open:

- doors
- dialogue options
- boss mercy routes
- secret endings
- alternate dungeon solutions
- map annotations
- companion insights

### Divine Blessings

Blessings are not simple stat buffs. They change how the player relates to the world.

Examples:

- **Hestia**: safe rest at restored hearths.
- **Poseidon**: calmer seas and hidden currents.
- **Artemis**: animals stop fearing the player.
- **Apollo**: false walls shimmer in sunlight.
- **Hades**: speak to the dead at graves.
- **Athena**: ancient mechanisms gain optional logic hints.
- **Hephaestus**: damaged devices can be repaired instead of destroyed.
- **Zeus**: storm pylons accept the player's charge.

### Mastery

Late-game progression combines relics.

Examples:

- Hestia flame plus Poseidon tide creates steam lifts.
- Apollo light plus Hephaestus mirrors activates solar engines.
- Hades obol plus Lyre of Dawn restores dead songs.
- Zeus lightning plus bronze machinery raises sky bridges.
- Artemis moon paths plus Underworld bridges reveal hidden sanctums.

## Progression Arc

### Early Game

- local swordplay
- simple relic use
- village-scale stakes
- visible secrets just out of reach
- first Echo scenes

### Mid Game

- sea travel
- multiple dungeon routes
- historical contradictions
- companion arcs
- advanced combat techniques
- language-based discoveries

### Late Game

- open world interconnection
- Underworld and sky routes
- optional truth hunting
- moral decisions with mechanical consequences
- Primordial sanctums

### Final Game

The player decides not only who wins, but what kind of reality continues.
