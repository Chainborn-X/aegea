# Region Breakdown

## Helikon Vale

Overview: Starting region of villages, ruins, river caves, pastoral mystery, and buried memory.

Towns:

- **Elia**: fishing and farming village.
- **Myrsos Mill**: river settlement with old songs and waterwheels.

Dungeons:

- Hearthwood Shrine.

Mini dungeons:

- Cliffside Tomb.
- Old Aqueduct.
- Shepherd's Cave.

Secret areas:

- Pactbearer crypt beneath Elia.
- Hestia's buried hearth.

NPC populations:

- fishers
- shepherds
- millers
- shrine keepers
- children who know old rhymes

Enemies:

- thorn wights
- cracked urn spirits
- feral shades
- root-bitten boars

Wildlife:

- foxes
- owls
- goats
- river turtles

Bosses:

- **The Ashen Keeper**, a hearth guardian corrupted by abandonment.

Side stories:

- Repairing the village bell reveals memories of the Sundering.
- Helping a widow recover her husband's name unlocks Underworld dialogue later.

## Thalassion Sea

Overview: Open sea hub unlocked after Act I, full of islands, drowned ruins, sea roads, and storm gates.

Towns:

- **Pyrgos Harbor**: shipwright town.
- **Melos-on-the-Waves**: floating market.

Dungeons:

- Temple of the Drowned Trident.

Mini dungeons:

- Shipwreck of the Dawn King.
- Coral Catacombs.
- Storm Buoy Tower.

Secret areas:

- Moonlit Reef.
- Sunken Oracle Court.
- Cave of Singing Shells.

NPC populations:

- sailors
- pearl divers
- storm priests
- mapmakers

Enemies:

- brine shades
- reef crawlers
- siren echoes
- drowned hoplites

Wildlife:

- dolphins
- gulls
- rays
- luminous fish

Bosses:

- **Ketos of the Deep Bell**, a sea beast drawn to forgotten worship.

Side stories:

- Charting old sea roads reveals lost islands.
- A sailor seeks the voice of his drowned sister.

## Nysa Wilds

Overview: Forest region governed by old boundaries, hunter law, animal pacts, and moon rites.

Towns:

- **Lykai Village**: hunters who refuse iron.
- **Selene Glade**: hidden moon shrine settlement.

Dungeons:

- Moonroot Labyrinth.

Mini dungeons:

- Hunter's Trial Hollow.
- Webbed Cedar Grove.
- The Antler Gate.

Secret areas:

- White Stag Path.
- Grove of Unfired Arrows.
- The village that vanished from maps.

NPC populations:

- hunters
- herbalists
- masked dancers
- children raised by forest law

Enemies:

- vine harriers
- moon wolves
- hollow dryads
- spider seers

Wildlife:

- deer
- lynx
- owls
- bees
- silver moths

Bosses:

- **Kallisto's Remnant**, a bear-shaped grief Echo.

Side stories:

- Choose whether to return a child to human village life or let them remain with forest kin.
- Restore a broken pact between hunters and animals.

## Daedalan Isles

Overview: Technology, craft, wind, gliding, and abandoned invention.

Towns:

- **Ankyra**: bronze harbor of artisans.
- **Bellows Rest**: forge village built around a dormant furnace.

Dungeons:

- The Bronze Labyrinth.

Mini dungeons:

- Clockwork Aviary.
- Mirror Foundry.
- Broken Lift Tower.

Secret areas:

- Hephaestus's private forge.
- Workshop of the First Automaton.
- Hidden schematic room for the Aegis Meridian.

NPC populations:

- smiths
- inventors
- apprentices
- automaton caretakers

Enemies:

- bronze crawlers
- furnace imps
- gear sentries
- failed automatons

Wildlife:

- cliff swallows
- goats
- ember beetles

Bosses:

- **Talos Fragment**, a colossal broken guardian.

Side stories:

- Help an automaton decide whether memory makes it alive.
- Reignite a forge without restoring its weapon-making function.

## Kartha Nemea

Overview: Dead heroic capital and red-grass ruins where public myth conceals betrayal.

Towns:

- **Nemean Camp**: archaeologists, mercenaries, and relic hunters.
- **Liongate Refuge**: descendants of the old city.

Dungeons:

- The Lion Vault.

Mini dungeons:

- Collapsed Arena.
- Royal Cistern.
- Hero's False Tomb.

Secret areas:

- Guardian beast shrine.
- Archive of erased kings.
- Underground mural of the first Pactbearers.

NPC populations:

- scholars
- refugees
- treasure hunters
- oathbound guards

Enemies:

- lion shades
- armored skeletons
- oathbreakers
- red-grass stalkers

Wildlife:

- jackals
- hawks
- desert hares

Bosses:

- **The Nemean Guardian**, not evil, but maddened by betrayal.

Side stories:

- Expose or preserve the comforting lie of the city's founding hero.
- Recover a child's ancestral crest from the false tomb.

## Hyperion Desert

Overview: Sun, prophecy, glass, buried truth, and the danger of knowing too much.

Towns:

- **Solon's Rest**: oasis town of scribes and merchants.
- **Aletheia Camp**: forbidden historians.

Dungeons:

- The Sunken Library of Apollo.

Mini dungeons:

- Mirage Well.
- Glass Orchard.
- Sundial Mausoleum.

Secret areas:

- Future-script archive.
- Observatory of broken constellations.
- The silent oracle chamber.

NPC populations:

- scribes
- water sellers
- glassmakers
- astrologers
- exiled priests

Enemies:

- sun wraiths
- glass scorpions
- mirage doubles
- prophecy masks

Wildlife:

- lizards
- beetles
- hawks
- desert foxes

Bosses:

- **The Blind Oracle**, a prophet split across possible futures.

Side stories:

- Decide whether to give a town an accurate prophecy of disaster.
- Reconstruct a poem that changes depending on time of day.

## Acheron Marsh and Underways

Overview: Death, memory, grief, reconciliation, and living routes into the Underworld.

Towns:

- **Nekyra Ferryhold**: village of ferrymen.
- **Mneme Rest**: settlement where living and dead briefly meet.

Dungeons:

- Palace Beneath the River.

Mini dungeons:

- Bone Orchard.
- Nameless Barrow.
- The Three Locks of Lethe.

Secret areas:

- The player's first grave.
- Hades's silent garden.
- A market where the dead trade memories.

NPC populations:

- ferrymen
- mourners
- shades
- grave singers

Enemies:

- Lethe drifters
- mourning masks
- grave moth swarms
- oath shades

Wildlife:

- frogs
- eels
- white cranes
- black butterflies

Bosses:

- **The Eater of Names**, a beast born from forgotten dead.

Side stories:

- Restore names to graves for permanent world changes.
- Let a ghost move on or remain as a village guardian.

## Atlas Spine

Overview: Mountain austerity, storms, forbidden hymns, and final pilgrimage.

Towns:

- **Eaglewatch Monastery**.
- **Keraunos Gate**: storm miners and pilgrims.

Dungeons:

- The Storm Crown.

Mini dungeons:

- Frozen Titan Rib.
- Lightning Chapel.
- Avalanche Shrine.

Secret areas:

- Zeus's first altar.
- Cave of forbidden hymns.
- Sky bridge to an optional Primordial sanctum.

NPC populations:

- monks
- climbers
- storm miners
- penitents

Enemies:

- thunder shades
- frost harpies
- armored eagles
- stone giants

Wildlife:

- mountain goats
- eagles
- snow hares

Bosses:

- **The First Thunder**, Zeus's abandoned wrath.

Side stories:

- Help monks decide whether to preserve or release dangerous hymns.
- Rescue a pilgrim who came seeking a god who no longer answers.
