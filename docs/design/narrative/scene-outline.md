# Scene-by-Scene Game Script Structure

This file is a blueprint for eventual script writing. It defines chapter purpose, gameplay purpose, major events, characters, locations, and boss encounters without locking final dialogue.

## Chapter 1 - The Star in the Hearthwood

Narrative purpose: Introduce home, dreams, first mystery.

Gameplay purpose: Basic movement, combat, first dungeon.

Major events:

- dream of Olympus gate
- star falls
- village attacked
- Hearthwood Shrine opens
- player gains Ember of Hestia

Characters involved: Hero, Demeas, village NPCs, Hestia Echo.

Locations visited: Elia, Hearthwood Shrine.

Boss encounter: Ashen Keeper.

Exploration sequence: Return to Elia with the Ember and discover one previously invisible memory route.

## Chapter 2 - The Road That Remembers

Narrative purpose: Establish that ruins preserve truth.

Gameplay purpose: Overworld exploration and first mini dungeons.

Major events:

- explore Helikon Vale
- find Pactbearer crypt
- meet Ione
- discover soul-mark

Characters involved: Hero, Ione, Demeas.

Locations visited: Helikon Vale, Cliffside Tomb, Myrsos Mill.

Boss encounter: Root Warden mini boss.

Exploration sequence: Use shrine flames to reveal hidden roads and early myth fragments.

## Chapter 3 - The Drowned Bell

Narrative purpose: Expand world to sea and lost civilizations.

Gameplay purpose: Sailing, tide puzzles, sea combat.

Major events:

- meet Phaon
- obtain ship
- sail to Thalassion ruins
- hear drowned bell
- defeat Ketos

Characters involved: Hero, Phaon, Poseidon Echo.

Locations visited: Pyrgos Harbor, Thalassion Sea, Drowned Trident Temple.

Boss encounter: Ketos of the Deep Bell.

Exploration sequence: Chart optional islands and discover the first unreachable sunken city view.

## Chapter 4 - The Knight of the Old Gods

Narrative purpose: Introduce ideological rival.

Gameplay purpose: First rival encounter and route gating.

Major events:

- meet Lysandra
- she demands relics be returned to temples
- duel ends unresolved
- gate to Nysa opens

Characters involved: Hero, Lysandra, Ione.

Locations visited: Old Temple Road.

Boss encounter: Lysandra duel.

Exploration sequence: First player choice in how to respond to divine restoration rhetoric.

## Chapter 5 - The Forest That Refused the Axe

Narrative purpose: Explore civilization versus wildness.

Gameplay purpose: Stealth, tracking, moon mechanics.

Major events:

- enter Nysa Wilds
- learn hunter pact is broken
- follow white stag
- defeat Kallisto's Remnant
- receive Moonbow Charm

Characters involved: Hero, Ione, Artemis Echo, Lykai villagers.

Locations visited: Nysa Wilds, Moonroot Labyrinth.

Boss encounter: Kallisto's Remnant.

Exploration sequence: Revisit earlier routes to find moon doors and animal-guided secrets.

## Chapter 6 - The Workshop of Broken Wings

Narrative purpose: Reveal mortals helped shape divine catastrophe.

Gameplay purpose: Machinery puzzles, gauntlet, gliding.

Major events:

- reach Daedalan Isles
- explore Bronze Labyrinth
- find Meridian schematics
- fight Talos Fragment
- unlock automaton side story

Characters involved: Hero, Ione, Hephaestus Echo, automaton NPC.

Locations visited: Ankyra, Bronze Labyrinth.

Boss encounter: Talos Fragment.

Exploration sequence: Use gliding and machinery repair to connect island routes.

## Chapter 7 - The Lion and the Lie

Narrative purpose: Challenge heroic myth.

Gameplay purpose: Strength puzzles, arena trials, archive discovery.

Major events:

- arrive at Kartha Nemea
- hear official hero legend
- discover guardian betrayal
- choose whether to reveal truth publicly
- gain Lionheart Aegis

Characters involved: Hero, Ione, Lysandra, refugees.

Locations visited: Kartha Nemea, Lion Vault.

Boss encounter: Nemean Guardian.

Exploration sequence: Decode conflicting myth fragments to open the true archive.

## Chapter 8 - The Library Beneath the Sun

Narrative purpose: Prophecy versus freedom.

Gameplay purpose: Light, shadow, mirage puzzles.

Major events:

- cross Hyperion Desert
- meet exiled historians
- Apollo reveals fractured futures
- player sees possible endings
- gain Lyre of Dawn

Characters involved: Hero, Ione, Apollo Echo, Blind Oracle.

Locations visited: Solon's Rest, Sunken Library.

Boss encounter: Blind Oracle.

Exploration sequence: Revisit sun-shadow locks at different times of day.

## Chapter 9 - The River of Names

Narrative purpose: Reveal death as memory, not evil.

Gameplay purpose: Underworld traversal, spirit dialogue.

Major events:

- enter Acheron Marsh
- restore ferry routes
- meet Hades
- discover player's first grave
- learn the true cause of the Sundering

Characters involved: Hero, Hades, Ione, dead heroes.

Locations visited: Nekyra Ferryhold, Palace Beneath the River.

Boss encounter: Eater of Names.

Exploration sequence: Restore names across the world to alter Underworld routes.

## Chapter 10 - The King Who Would Replace Heaven

Narrative purpose: Human ambition becomes antagonist.

Gameplay purpose: Assault on fortress, mixed enemy factions.

Major events:

- Melanthos activates Meridian weapons
- Lysandra may ally or oppose player depending on choices
- player recovers Broken Meridian Shard
- Olympus gate begins to appear

Characters involved: Hero, Melanthos, Lysandra, Ione.

Locations visited: Meridian Bastion.

Boss encounter: Melanthos's divine war engine.

Exploration sequence: Meridian devices alter previous regions and open late-game routes.

## Chapter 11 - The Storm Crown

Narrative purpose: Confront divine authority before Olympus.

Gameplay purpose: Mountain traversal, lightning mechanics.

Major events:

- climb Atlas Spine
- monks reveal forbidden hymns
- face Zeus's abandoned wrath
- gain Bolt of Keraunos
- path to Olympus opens

Characters involved: Hero, Ione, Lysandra, Zeus Echo.

Locations visited: Atlas Spine, Storm Crown.

Boss encounter: The First Thunder.

Exploration sequence: Activate storm pylons and sky bridges across the world.

## Chapter 12 - The World Remembers

Narrative purpose: Open final exploration phase.

Gameplay purpose: World transformation and optional truth completion.

Major events:

- ancient roads rise
- new islands surface
- Underworld and sky routes connect
- secret bosses and endings become available
- companion arcs resolve

Characters involved: Full cast.

Locations visited: Entire world.

Boss encounters: Optional world bosses and secret bosses.

Exploration sequence: Myth hunting, Primordial sanctums, dead-name restoration, and final ending alignment.

## Chapter 13 - Olympus Broken

Narrative purpose: Final reckoning with gods and history.

Gameplay purpose: Final dungeon and all-relic integration.

Major events:

- enter divine memory palaces
- witness gods' failures
- confront Lysandra or Melanthos if unresolved
- reach Aegis Meridian
- meet Zeus the Unremembered

Characters involved: Hero, Zeus, Athena, Hestia, Hades, Ione, Lysandra, Aion.

Locations visited: Olympus Broken.

Boss encounter: Zeus the Unremembered.

Exploration sequence: Solve final memory chambers based on how much truth the player uncovered.

## Chapter 14 - The Choice of the Age

Narrative purpose: Decide the future.

Gameplay purpose: Ending path culmination.

Major events:

- Aion offers recurrence
- player chooses restoration, new pact, end of gods, or secret Eighth Echo
- world epilogue changes based on side stories, truths found, and companion arcs

Characters involved: Hero, Aion, gods, companions, world NPCs.

Locations visited: Summit of Names.

Boss encounter: Aion's Trial.

Exploration sequence: None after the final decision unless post-game is added later.
