# Combat

## Combat Role

Combat should create danger and mastery without consuming the identity of the game. Aegea is an exploration-first action RPG.

## Feel

Combat should be readable, physical, and responsive. Encounters should work at top-down scale with clear telegraphs, concise enemy behaviors, and room for movement.

## Encounter Principles

- Enemies should fit their habitat.
- Combat spaces should preserve environmental identity.
- Not every screen needs enemies.
- Bosses and guardians should be tied to place, myth, or access.
- Avoid random-feeling enemy placement.

## Player Growth

Combat progression may include:

- New weapon forms.
- Relic abilities.
- Defensive tools.
- Movement options.
- Blessings tied to exploration.
- Environmental tactics.

## Tone

Combat can be dangerous and tense, but it should not turn the world grim or cruel as the default.
