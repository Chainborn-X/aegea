# Rendering

## Rendering Goals

Rendering should support the art pillars:

- Warm sunlight.
- Atmospheric depth.
- Vibrant water.
- Painterly textures.
- Readable top-down gameplay.
- Large environmental forms that make the player feel small.

## Early Needs

- Layered 2D scene composition.
- Directional light language, even if baked into art.
- Water surfaces and reflections.
- Vegetation movement or implied motion.
- Fog, haze, and depth cues.
- Readable shadows and collision boundaries.

## Risks

- Effects that reduce gameplay readability.
- Lighting systems that clash with hand-painted art.
- Overuse of darkness in caves and Underworld regions.
- Asset workflows that make iteration too slow.
